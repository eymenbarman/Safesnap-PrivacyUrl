SafeSnap – Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 8, 2025

SafeSnap (“App”, “we”, “our”) is a mobile application that helps detect safety risks in rooms for children and elderly. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and how we protect it.

Information We Collect

  • User-uploaded photos – processed temporarily on our servers only to generate a safety analysis. Photos are not stored permanently and are deleted after processing.
  • Purchase and subscription data – obtained via Google Play Billing and RevenueCat, used solely to provide Pro features.
  • Error logs and basic usage metrics (optional) – may be collected to improve app stability.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Billing – in-app purchases and subscriptions.
  • RevenueCat – subscription and entitlement management.
  • Image hosting / AI providers (if used) – such as ImgBB or OpenAI, only for analysis purposes.

Data Use & Retention

  • Photos are deleted after analysis; no permanent storage.
  • Purchase records may be stored to prevent fraud and meet legal requirements.
  • Error logs are typically retained for 30 days.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Information is only shared with third-party services listed above to enable core functionality.

Security

All communication is encrypted via HTTPS. Sensitive keys and secrets are stored securely. Access control and logging are applied.

Your Rights

  • You may choose not to upload photos.
  • You can request deletion of any data stored on our servers.

Children’s Privacy

SafeSnap is designed to be used by parents/guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.
